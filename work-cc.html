Design - Flash Tournament

Brief:

Increasing user engagement level and ARPPU.

Flash tournament mode where the winner is determined immediately after a fixed number of players has participated in the tournament (instead of waiting for 24 hours). This is a strong feature request according to our recent user survey. Such a tournament format has much more prompt feedback to the player than our current fix-duration tournament format, thus increasing their engagement level and ARPPU.

Design - Challenge Shout Out

Brief:

As a way to initiate social interactions between players, social challenge has been a very welcomed feature, especially for real money players. About 26% real-money players have proposed social challenges and 7% real-money matches as challenge matches. We also got feedback from loyal users that they want more personal interactions with other players.
